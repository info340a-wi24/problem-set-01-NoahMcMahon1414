Steven Universe

This page is all about the best character from a Cartoon Network show, Steven Universe. Steven is the main character in the show Website, where he lives with the Crystal Gems on the beach. We follow the life of Steven as he constantly saves his town from rogue gems that are trying to hurt his family and friends. Steven is amazing because he:

  • has a bubble shield,
  • is always positive,
  • and has magical healing powers.
